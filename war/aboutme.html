If you are a fan of Bryan Adams and heard Summer of '69 then I can describe my tenure at university in his words, "those were the best days of my life". 4 years at the hostels, staying awake late night for assignments, labs till 7 in the evening, studying hard when exams were knocking at the door... otherwise fooling around with roommates & friends and having lots of fun... are just unforgettable memories. I was fortunate to have some awesome roommates... one was fundoo... one was always afraid... one was always forgetful...we were and are still friends.

I am studying Master of Science in Computer Science @Texas A & M University, College Station, Texas, USA. I have joined TAMU in Fall 2014 and expect to graduate by Fall 2016.
I have also completed an executive program in Global Business Management from Indian Institute of Management, Calcutta.

After that I moved to Cognizant Technology Solutions (CTS). Well... I met some of my best colleagues and closest friends during my career... full of fun and enthusiam... enjoyed working together on challenging projects and at the same time had immense fun... rarely tensed even in the midst of tough situations, deadlines...which earned me the title of "chillax" from my buddies.
